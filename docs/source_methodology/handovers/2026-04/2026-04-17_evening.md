new_findings:

  SW23_utc_ny_parquet_boundary:
    severity: "latent / LOW today, MEDIUM if KZ scopes extend"
    surfaced_during: "SW02 + SW09 test fixture design"
    description: |
      River parquet files are keyed by UTC calendar date, but
      map_orchestrator's per-date init and SW09's recovery both filter
      bars by NY date. For a session_date td, the Oct 1 NY evening
      bars (20:00-23:59 NY) live in the Oct 2 UTC parquet file.
      Replay filters must therefore load an extra UTC day to capture
      the full NY day — otherwise bar coverage is truncated to
      ~19:00 UTC on td.
    current_impact: |
      ZERO — LOKZ (02:00-05:00 NY) and NYOKZ (07:00-10:00 NY) both fall
      well before the NY evening window. Chain + trade decisions happen
      before any bar gets truncated.
    future_hazard: |
      If KZ scopes ever extend into the 19:00-23:59 NY evening window,
      or if a new Asia-window-aligned strategy reads this range, the
      UTC/NY boundary becomes a correctness bug.
    proposed_fix: |
      Helper in data/river.py: load_ny_day_bars(d) that fetches
      [d UTC, d+1 UTC] and filters by NY date. Callers migrate
      incrementally.
    block_2_entry: "added to M3.5 block_2 in FORWARD_PLAN.md v1.4"

  SW24_ra_engine_naive_datetime:
